6. Applicability

 

 

The uses of ANNs are far reaching and interdisciplinary

Using artificial neural networks, we can greatly reduce the time spent on having radiologists identify between benign and malignant microcalcifications and with better accuracy regarding early detection of breast cancer in mammograms. This, of course, is applicable to other fields of medical science as well and is not contained only to cancer research (paper)

Facial recognition

Artificial neural networks can also aid in facial recognition using the same process that we have on a much larger scale, although it can be limited by the amount of enrolled users in the database that you are matching with (paper)

Object detection

Neural networks can further be applied to not just 2D images, but also to real life objects for collision avoidance and obstacle detection when designing self- driving vehicles (article)

Robotics

We can also use this to better sort recyclables instead of relying on human eye detection. The speed of which recyclables are sorted can also increase with the implementation of the convolutional neural network, which we mentioned was better suited to image recognition than an artificial neural network (article, paper).
